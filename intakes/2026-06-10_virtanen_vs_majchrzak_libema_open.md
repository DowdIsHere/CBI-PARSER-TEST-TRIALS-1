- **Tiebreak Trouble:** Majchrzak. Lacking an unreturnable serve, he struggles in tiebreaks against elite power — lost two of three tiebreaks to Virtanen in Birmingham on raw opponent power.
- **Physical Fatigue in Slams:** Not Applicable — ATP 250 (Libéma Open), best-of-three.
