# CBI Parser Sourcing Protocol
**How a Parser is placed BEFORE it reaches the read engine. The read engine never sources its own Parser; it consumes a placement produced here. Consultation produces the placement; the human declares it; the declared placement with its provenance is the frozen input to the read.**

---

## Roles

- **The human (Robert) declares the placement.** The final Parser call is his, not the model's.
- **The model runs the instrument.** It scores axes against the Parser Print Identifier (Part 1 governs), flags contamination, runs disambiguation pairs, holds the contradiction-blocks rule, and names the tier. It surfaces "here is where the evidence points and at what tier" — it does NOT declare the verdict as settled and does NOT pre-empt the human's call.
- The model may disagree, and should, with reasons. But it brings evidence to a decision point; it does not place.

## The defended-source rule (from the Playbook Core Rule)

The Parser is fixed and cannot drift. A placement is legitimate only if it defeats the conditions-confound by one of three mechanisms:
- **Control** — the Parser Profile™ assessment (confound removed by construction).
- **Volume across variation** — the reflexive behavioral layer read across enough varied conditions (e.g. different surfaces) that conditions cancel and the fixed print remains.
- **Independence** — cross-domain triangulation: agreement across uncorrelated domains (play AND speech AND habit). Conditions can bend one domain but cannot coordinate several into a false agreement.

A single confounded slice — one match, one stretch, a stylistic stereotype, or stats/ranking — is NEVER a legitimate source. It has no defense against the confound.

## Part 1 governs

The universal print layer (Part 1) is domain-independent and authoritative. Domain chapters (tennis, etc.) are projections of Part 1 onto an expression surface. **If a domain chapter conflicts with Part 1, Part 1 wins.** A print read off how the player behaves in one sport can be systematically inverted by that sport's demands (e.g. the Abstract family gets taxed in tennis and can look Concrete) — so score the universal layer, and treat domain behavior as one contaminated surface among several, never as the sole source.

## The consultation procedure

1. **Collect across at least two independent domains** before scoring anything. Speech, reflexive play, off-court habit, career structure. One domain is PROVISIONAL at best.
2. **Score each axis independently** — Spatial, Temporal, Reference — against Part 1's universal tells. Never let one axis pull another.
3. **Weight reflexive over deliberate.** Trained media answers, rehearsed lines, coaching-register optimism are the deliberate layer and can mimic any orientation. The print shows in the off-script residual. (This is why a player's forward-looking interview talk is not by itself a Future tell.)
4. **Use disconfirming tells.** What the person reliably does NOT do is as diagnostic as what they do. Low Generative Creation (drains chaos, repeats patterns, no novel invention) rules out the Abstract-Future-vision branch regardless of how "tactical" the player is — archived/applied tactics are Pattern Recognition, not Generative Creation; only NOVEL, unprecedented invention is GC.
5. **Run the disambiguation pairs** on any axis where two adjacent orientations both seem live (e.g. Sharp vs. Seasoned on Reference: does the player archive and adjust to the opponent — Seasoned — or play the ball and pattern only, not the person — Sharp).
6. **Check the four contamination sources:** conditions mimicry, role masks, coaching masks, social desirability.
7. **Resolve contradictions, do not average.** A contradiction blocks placement until resolved by finding the role mask or the mis-scored tell. Forcing a placement through a contradiction is the most common identification error.
8. **Assign a tier** (below) and **record the evidence, not just the conclusion** — every placement must be auditable back to its tells and dated.

## Confidence tiers

- **PROVISIONAL** — one domain only, or fewer than three independent observations. Hypothesis-grade.
- **CORROBORATED** — two independent domains agree, no surviving contradiction. Working read, flagged as such.
- **LOCKED** — three+ independent domains agree, zero unresolved contradictions, observations span time. Only LOCKED placements are tracker-grade.

## Hand-off to the read engine

The declared placement travels to the read as a **provenance record**: the profile (e.g. Concrete · Past · Balanced — Seasoned), the sourcing method, the tier, the domains/evidence, and the date. The read engine consumes this as given; it does not re-derive, adjudicate, or cap on it. The tier is for the human's own records and declaration decisions — any consequence of a sub-LOCKED tier (e.g. treating a read as working-grade rather than tracker-grade) is applied by the human at declaration or logging time, never enforced by the read engine.

## Worked precedent: Hugo Grenier

Run as the template for this protocol. Two domains (speech + tactical/behavioral). Initial reads toward Foresighted then Visionary (both Abstract · Future, both requiring HIGH Generative Creation) were FALSIFIED by the disconfirming GC tell: the player drains chaos, repeats disciplined patterns, and his "novelty" is body-type irony (a tall man who grinds), not invented shot-making — the opposite of GC. Forward-looking interview talk was discarded as coaching-register per step 3. Landed at **Concrete · Past · Balanced — SEASONED**, on the Seasoned signature of archiving the opponent and adjusting from memory (high opponent-PR, unlike Sharp). Tier: **CORROBORATED** — not LOCKED; needs a third time-spanning domain (off-court habits), and the Self-vs-Balanced axis, while leaning Balanced, is not airtight. This is a working read, not yet a tracker-grade Parser input.
